Timeline

When Where What

2013-14 was yet another wholly happening year with the following being the major events.

  • August 8

    Yahoo! HackU

    Join Yahoo! Web experts for four days of learning, hacking and fun! You’ll hear interesting tech talks, hacking tips and lessons, and get hands on coding workshops where you’ll work with cutting-edge technology. This will be followed by an opportunity to build your dream apps in our 24 hours hackathon!

  • August 13

    Fresher's Welcome

    The welcome party for CS facchas! High on Entertainment, masti and khana!!

  • September 12

    Number Games

    The perfect day to show off your puzzle solving skills. Battle it out with Sudokus, Kakuros, Hittoris, Slitherlinks, and more! And of course, the winner will get a cash prize.

  • September 26

    Frosh Welcome

    A brief introduction on important aspects of CS like WebD, PS, GCL accounts, Linux, Video Editing etc.

  • November 10

    Futsal Knockout Tournament

    The CS department football tournament! This is the oppurtunity for the footballers of the department to show off their skills. There are lucrative prizes for the best teams. And yes of course loads of refreshments for everyone.

  • January 10-12

    Code.Fun.Do

    Students compete in this event,conducted in association with Microsoft, to create Windows apps. Free food for everyone, and great prizes for the best apps!

  • January 24

    Treasure Hunt

    Lots of mind numbing clues were spread over the Bharti Building and exciting prizes for the fastest to get to the treasure.

  • February 15

    Google DevFestX

    An event organised in collaboration with the Google Student Ambassador Program for students who loves to code and have a great idea for a mobile app. A 2-day hackathon with exciting prizes, free meals and regular snacks.

  • February 26 - March 1

    Tryst

    The tech-fest of IIT-Delhi. ACES-ACM conducted a host of novel online as well as offline competitions during the 3 day long event.

  • March 12

    Innovation Anytime, Anyplace by Anybody- A talk by the inventor of e-mail

    A marvellous talk given by V.A. Shiva Ayyadurai, who is not only an entrepeneur, scientist-technologist and educator, but also the inventor of e-mail.

  • March 15

    Tennis Ball Cricket Tournament

    We kickstart the second half of the semester with some cross-departmental cricket!

  • March 29

    Asian Programming Contest 2014

    The Asia-level coding competition is aimed at bringing out the best programmers and fostering brotherhood among the participating countries. More than 400 teams from 7 Asian countries fought it out for the top spot.

  • April 1st Week

    Job ke fundae by final year students

    A golden oppurtunity for those in there penultimate year to learn from their seniors' experiences.

  • April 3

    Double Trouble - Tech Quiz

    The annual Tech Quiz. Expect questions that will make you blast off to the world of technology and give you a chance to win on-the-spot cash prizes!

  • April 16

    CounterStrike Tournament

    The name says it all! Get a chance to win exciting cash prizes and score some free food, all while playing your favorite game. It doesn't get better than this.
