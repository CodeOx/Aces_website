ACES

ACES is the students organization of the Computer Science department of Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. ACES encourages cultural and technical collaboration and interaction among the students of the department.

Our Goal

Our main goals include increasing co-operation and fraternity across years in the department and ensuring that all students feel welcome in a healthy and inclusive atmosphere at IIT Delhi.

Our Process

Our functioning is completely in the hands of students under the aegis of faculty. We organize various competitive events, workshops, sporting tournaments and interaction sessions over the year.
